Dr. Kenneth Reid

Clinical Psychologist

Dr. Kenneth Reid is a seasoned trader and trading coach with a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology. He was the Senior Editor and Head Trader at one of the largest financial newsletters in the U.S. Dr Reid ran a daily swing trading service and several theme-based portfolios for 12 years, navigating two bear markets, including the GFC.

Since then he has focused full-time on coaching daytraders of all levels of skill, experience and account size. He treats newbies with the same consideration and respect as 7-figure prop shop traders.

Dr. Reid has an abiding interest in the technical side of trading and believes that mindset and technical skill should always be addressed together. An avid tennis player, he sees many parallels with that sport and applies the principles of modern sports psychology to trading. Dr Reid was a qualifier at the USTA Nationals in 2018. He’s still working on his serve.

Jared Tendler

Performance Coach

Jared Tendler, MS, LMHC, is a leading expert in how your mental game impacts performance. His clients include world champion poker players, several PGA Tour players, entrepreneurs and hundreds of institutional and independent financial traders. Jared holds a master’s degree in Counseling Psychology and attained a license as a mental health counselor. With over 18 years of experience coaching people from around the world and in multiple arenas, his straightforward and logical approach has been proven to help people solve their mental game problems and perform at their highest levels.

He is the author of three highly acclaimed books, The Mental Game of Poker 1 & 2 and his newest book The Mental Game of Trading. He also previously served as Head of Sport Psychology for the esports organization Team Liquid. Jared’s diverse experience and proven techniques make him among the best mental coaches in the world.

Rande Howell

Trading Psychologist

Rande Howell is a performance coach and licensed therapist. His work focuses on using emotions to build an effective mind for the management of uncertainty. Rande’s work is grounded in Emotional Intelligence, Mindfulness, and developing latent potential in investors and traders. He teaches them how to harness emotions to achieve the performance edge so they can effectively control the mind that is brought into the moment of managing uncertainty, rather than attempting to control the outcome.

Based in the United States, he teaches and trains internationally for the emotional finance community. His publications have appeared in many venues and he is the author of the book, Mindful Trading: Mastering Your Emotions and the Inner Game.
