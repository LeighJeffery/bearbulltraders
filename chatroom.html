Frequently Asked Questions

In the chatroom, traders can ask questions, discuss current market conditions, and interact with other traders in a friendly environment. You can watch Andrew and Brian trading at the market open, real-time, with other moderators sharing their trades, reasoning, and strategies throughout the day.

The BBT chatroom follows a schedule that aligns with the NYSE trading days. It opens at 8 am and concludes at 4 pm, coinciding with the market close. The chatroom operates from Monday to Friday, and it observes NYSE trading days and holidays.

You can access the chatrooms through a web browser like Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox on your computer. If you're using an iOS device like an iPhone or iPad, use Safari, and for Android devices, use Firefox. Alternatively, you can use a mobile app to access the chatroom.

All membership levels grant access to the chatroom. Additionally, you can experience our chatroom with a 7-day trial through the Intro membership.
